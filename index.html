• 214 g all-purpose flour
  • 16 g baking powder
  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  • Combine flour and baking powder in a large bowl. Add the butter and mix with fingertips until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. Stir in sugar and fruit.
  • Break the egg into a measuring cup and make up to 1/2 cup with milk. Stir egg and milk into the mix.
  • Roll the dough out to 1/2 inch thickness. Cut into triangles or into circles with a cutter. Brush the tops of the scones with milk.
  • Bake for about 10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool the scones on a wire rack.
Special Fruit Scones

From Mary Berry's Baking Bible

Makes 14 scones
